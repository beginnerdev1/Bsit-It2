COLLEGE VISION

A globally – recognized Scientific and Technological University by 2027

COLLEGE MISSION

ASCOT shall produce globally – competitive, ethically – upright,
and gender-responsive graduates in the fields of arts
and sciences, forestry, agriculture, education, information technology,
industrial technology, engineering, marine,
and environmental sciences by promoting quality instruction, research, extension,
and production towards sustainable development.
